About our Founder

Kate Doheny leads Doheny Atelier with over a decade of experience designing for beauty and lifestyle brands. She began her career at Oscar de la Renta, then moved to Dior Parfums before spending 7+ years at Coty, where she rebranded Kylie Cosmetics and designed more than 30 products for the brand. She has also created work for Adidas, Colgate-Palmolive, and other Fortune 500 companies.

Kate works across both luxury and mass markets, handling everything from packaging to activations, social, and experiential design. She has a particular focus on sustainability and on using consumer data to shape products that perform.
