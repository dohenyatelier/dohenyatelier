Our Mission

Doheny Atelier seeks to enhance the brand you have and build the brand you want (and deserve!) through artful and consumer-desired packaging and identity systems. With over a decade of industry expertise and a foundation in deep research, the Atelier believes your brand deserves to make a bold, impactful impression both on the shelf and out in the world. The Atelier collaborates with businesses at every stage, from emerging startups to established enterprise-level organizations.

Our Strategy

Doheny Atelier brings life to founders’ visions through building a collaborative design approach that pairs each client with discernible quality. Drawing on deep consumer research and more than 10 years of industry expertise, the Atelier uses proven methods and analytical insights to develop fresh concepts and revitalize your brand. Recognizing that modern consumers prioritize both scientific integrity and sustainability, the Atelier ensures every packaging solution reflects the quality of the formula within, creating identity systems that broadcast the inherent strength and data-driven foundation of your business.
