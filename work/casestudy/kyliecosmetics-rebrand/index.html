Kylie Cosmetics by Kylie Jenner Rebrand

Following its initial direct-to-consumer success, Kylie Cosmetics collaborated with Coty and founder of Doheny Atelier on a global portfolio refresh to introduce new product collections, expanding the brand into brick-and-mortar retail with a scalable, elevated visual identity.

About the Project

Kylie Cosmetics, the highly coveted celebrity-founded makeup brand, was founded in 2014 by influencer, mom, and among the top 10 most-followed people on Instagram, Kylie Jenner. The brand's mission was personal: she built the brand from her childhood insecurities about her lips and wanted her customers to feel beautiful. Kylie’s influence was in high demand: she sold out of her first product, the “Lip Kit”, within hours of launch. The brand was anchored in a DTC business setup for its first five years, with a US-only market.

Kate, founder of Doheny Atelier, led the entire portfolio refresh for Kylie Cosmetics’ debut at Coty, working closely with the brand’s founder, Kylie Jenner. As the influential brand has significantly grown, so has its founder. Kate created a new product collection to match Kylie’s newly elevated confidence and style, formed in Kylie’s visionary, of-the-moment approach. The launch also marked the brand's entry into the global economy and brick-and-mortar space. The visual language was framed in a recognizable, immediately expandable framework. The use of pink, the reliance on one uniform block logo syle, and silver touches throughout made it easily adaptable to launch displays, OOH activations, and even Kylie’s signature vending machine.

Kylie’s iconic pink creates a colorful backdrop for her lip products. Brand codes, including the newly drawn drips and uniform block letter logo center the products in the new visual identity.

02 Face

Although the signature pink hues and silver block lettering serve as primary brand codes, the collection’s nuanced details, such as the hidden dust covers within blush and bronzer compacts and the custom-embossed logo patterns on the highlighters bulk, deliver an elevated moment of surprise and delight.
