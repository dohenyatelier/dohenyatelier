Colgate Optic White Holiday Gift Sets

Colgate-Palmolive, a Fortune 500 powerhouse, dominates the oral-care market with its Optic White line. Mirroring the brand's promise of a radiant smile, Colgate's oral care division received an extensive, glamorous rebrand. As creative director for the Optic White holiday giftset collection, Doheny Atelier founder Kate embraced this sparkling new aesthetic, utilizing vibrant 3D product renderings to elevate the front of the packaging.

Client Colgate Optic White
Industry Oral Care
Personal Care
Services Creative Direction

About the Project

As a long-established legacy brand and a member of the Fortune 500, Colgate-Palmolive holds a prominent position within the oral-care industry. Optic White stands out as one of its most successful lines, providing a toothpaste brand that features various levels of clean and whitening solutions. Mirroring the bright, sparkling guarantees it provides for its customers' teeth, Colgate's oral care division has undergone extensive rebranding over the years.

Kate, the founder of Doheny Atelier, served as the creative director for the Colgate Optic White holiday giftset collection. She embraced the glamorous aspects of the rebrand, incorporating an element of sparkle and shine through graphic 3D product renderings featured on the front of the packaging.

  • DesignerAether NY

01 Optic White Purple Toothpaste and Overnight Whitening Pen Holiday Giftset

Building on the beautiful rebrand of Colgate Optic White packaging, showcasing the products directly on the front of the pack felt like a natural progression. A touch of whimsy is introduced through flowing ribbons woven throughout the design, while the iconic Optic White orb is playfully reimagined as a holiday ornament to tie this limited-edition giftset together — all while maintaining the unmistakable brand recognition and ownable elements.

02 Optic White Mini Overnight Whitening Pen Holiday Giftset

Mirroring the visual identity of the larger holiday set, the Mini Overnight Whitening Pen Holiday Giftset incorporates vibrant sparkles, elegant ribbons, and signature Colgate red branding to spread the holiday spirit. Carefully adapted for the smaller package scale, the creative layout highlights the product front and center, creating a playful and whimsical layout.
