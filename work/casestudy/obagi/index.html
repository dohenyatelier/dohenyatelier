About the Project

Obagi Medical is the global leader in professional-grade skincare, formed in 1988 by dermatologist Dr. Zein Obagi. Skincare experts certify the brand's efficacious and results-driven approach, with formulas backed by clinical testing. Since its founding, Obagi Medical has expanded into a wide portfolio of products across dedicated treatment spaces including Nu-Gen™, Professional-C®, and Clenziderm M.D®.

Kate, the founder of Doheny Atelier, led the refresh of the entire Obagi brand landscape. The transformation began with the logo, as Kate established a new, cohesive identity that consolidated several legacy Obagi logos into a single, powerful vision. This revitalized visual identity provided a compelling foundation for the packaging, establishing a strategic design hierarchy to modernize and unify the extensive portfolio of over 40 products for today's consumer.

01 Logo

Before the rebrand, Obagi Medical utilized approximately four or five inconsistent logos. To better serve a modern generation of skincare consumers while remaining familiar to longtime users, the brand required a unified and recognizable logo foundation.

02 Primary Packaging

Disrupting the shelf doesn't always require brand-new componentry. On-pack, the refreshed logo makes a bold statement, complemented by a new color-coded system that establishes a clear hierarchy to guide consumers through various skincare treatments.

03 Secondary Packaging

The use of bold white signals a medical-grade standard, providing a striking contrast that emphasizes the logo. On the full secondary packaging, key clinical statistics are showcased through an engaging layout designed to capture interest and tell the brand's story.
